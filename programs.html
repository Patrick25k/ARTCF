Our Programs

Discover how we're making a difference in communities in Rwanda.

Our Approach

At ARTCF, we believe in sustainable, community-driven solutions that address the root causes of challenges. Our programs are designed with and for the communities we serve in Rwanda, ensuring relevance, ownership, and long-term impact.

Education

Providing access to quality education and learning resources for women and children.

Economic Empowerment

Creating sustainable economic opportunities for Christian women workers.

Education Programs

Our education initiatives focus on providing quality learning opportunities for women and children in Rwanda.

Women's Literacy Program

We provide adult literacy classes for women who missed out on formal education, empowering them with reading and writing skills.

  • 2,000+ women educated since 1988
  • Basic numeracy and life skills
  • Community-based learning centers

Impact Stories

Real stories of change from the women we serve in Rwanda.

"A Partner called Bertilde Nyirankurikiyimana, mother of three children is living in Gishubi sector, Nyakibungo cell, Banga village. Before joining Kira Wigire program, it was very difficult for her to respond to all their needs in the family where her children dropped out the school. After participating in the training on Kira Wigire program organized by ARTCF in partnership with World Vision she changed in her mind and think about how to change also in her pocket. She got a loan from S4T group and with her saving from her IGA built her own house. She received also the first grant from World Vision and used it to bought chickens and goats. Another grant used to buy the second rice plantation and also paid School fees of her Children. "

"We SIBOMUREMYI Onesphore and MUKAKALISA Thacienne. were living in extreme poverty because of conflict in our family; we cannot found basic needs for satisfying our family and our Children were affected in convenience lacking their right, because conflict between me and my wife .After reaching by World Vision in partnership with ARTCF, Inshuti z’umuryango (IZU) and local authorities after participating in different trainings our mind set changed and now we are be able to solve our problems which can came in our family and then we are exemplary in our community because of Celebrating family sessions"
